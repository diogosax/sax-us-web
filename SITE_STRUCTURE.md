# SITE_STRUCTURE.md

Defines the official sitemap, page goals, content boundaries, and navigation structure for the **SAX US Website**.

This file is the source of truth for the website structure and must be followed by any AI agent or developer working on this repository.

---

## Project Goal

The website must present **SAX Group LLC** as a professional U.S.-based platform that helps Latin American companies expand into the United States.

The site should communicate:

- credibility
- clarity
- cross-border expertise
- operational support
- business-focused execution

This is a **marketing and informational website**.

It is not a web app or SaaS interface.

---

## Primary Navigation

The main navigation should include:

- Home
- Sax Global
- Our Companies
- About
- Contact

A primary CTA button may also appear in the header:

- Schedule a Consultation

Language switching should be supported in a simple way.

Recommended approach:

- English as default
- Portuguese version for Sax Global
- Optional language switch in the header: `EN | PT`

Do not create unnecessary pages beyond this structure unless explicitly approved.

---

## Sitemap

### 1. Home
**Route:** `/`

**Goal:**  
Quickly explain who SAX is, what it does, and why it matters.

**Suggested sections:**
- Hero
- What We Do
- Our Companies
- Why SAX
- Final CTA

**Content focus:**
- international expansion
- company setup
- legal/accounting/logistics support
- business credibility
- clear next step

- Core Services
- Who We Help
- Why Work With SAX Global
- CTA

**Content focus:**
- company formation

### 4. Our Companies

### 5. About

### 6. Contact

## Header Structure

Recommended header behavior:

- SAX logo on the left
- Navigation links in the center or right
- CTA button on the right
- Optional language switcher

Header should be:

- clean
- lightweight
- responsive
- easy to scan

---

## Footer Structure

Recommended footer should include:

- SAX logo
- brief company description
- navigation links
- company links
- contact link
- copyright

Optional:
- social links, only if officially provided

Do not invent social media profiles.

---

## Home Page Messaging Priorities

The homepage should communicate these ideas in order:

1. What SAX does
2. Who SAX helps
3. Why SAX is different
4. What companies are part of the ecosystem
5. How to contact SAX

If the homepage becomes too crowded, reduce visual complexity instead of adding more sections.

---

## Page Design Philosophy

All pages should feel:

- modern
- minimal
- credible
- startup-style
- business-focused

Avoid:

- clutter
- excessive text
- decorative sections without purpose
- generic corporate filler

---

## Content Boundaries

AI agents and developers must stay within the approved site scope.

Do not add:

- blog
- careers page
- pricing page
- testimonials
- case studies
- team page
- newsroom
- resource hub

unless explicitly approved later.
